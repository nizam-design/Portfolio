My story

Designing with clarity, driven by deeper thinking

I've always been drawn to business, psychology, and philosophy—understanding how people think, how systems work, and the meaning behind decisions. That curiosity naturally led me to UI/UX and product design.

I started with design fundamentals, but over time my focus shifted toward product thinking—solving real problems with clarity, not just creating visuals. Along the way, I've also taken on leadership responsibilities, including leading operations in logistics at Tata Motors, where I learned how to manage people, handle pressure, and make practical decisions.

Currently, I work as a UI/UX tutor while building my own portfolio and product ideas. Teaching has helped me strengthen my thinking and communicate design in a simple, structured way.

I’m especially interested in early-stage products where design, business, and user behavior intersect. My goal is to build products that are not just usable—but meaningful and impactful.
